About KrishiLens

KrishiLens is an AI-powered platform that helps identify Indian cattle and buffalo breeds. By uploading an image, our system recognizes the breed and provides insights.

This initiative supports farmers, researchers, and livestock enthusiasts by promoting awareness and conservation of native breeds.

How it Works

1. Upload Image

Select and upload your desired image file for processing.

2. AI Analysis

Our advanced artificial intelligence analyzes the content and characteristics of your image.

3. View Results

Access and review the detailed analysis and insights generated from your image.

Indian Breeds

Gir Cow

The Gir is a famous Indian dairy breed known for its high milk yield and resilience in hot climates.

Sahiwal Cow

The Sahiwal is one of the best dairy breeds, producing rich, high-fat milk.

Murrah Buffalo

Murrah buffaloes are famous for their high milk yield and adaptability across India.

Red Sindhi

Popular in India and Pakistan, Red Sindhi cows are excellent for crossbreeding and milk.

Rathi

A breed from Rajasthan, Rathi cows are hardy and produce good quantities of milk.

Kankrej

Native to Gujarat and Rajasthan, Kankrej cows are large, strong, and great for milk and work.

Ongole

Originating from Andhra Pradesh, Ongole cows are known for strength and milk production.

Hariana

Native to Haryana, Hariana cows are strong, disease-resistant, and valued for both milk and draught work.
